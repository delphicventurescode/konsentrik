# Mascot Images

This directory contains the visual identity for **KONSENTRIK**.

## The Mascot: "Quatre Visages"
The image featured here is a photograph of Pablo Picasso’s *Quatre Visages* (Four Faces), a jug created in 1959. 

### Why this image?
We chose this piece to represent KONSENTRIK because of its interconnected nature. Just as the faces on the jug share eyes and contours, the modules within KONSENTRIK share a common "shell," representing the holistic and integrated way we approach mental wellness and focus.
